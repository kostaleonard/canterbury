  1.  sealed trait Civilization extends AnyRef

    A civilization.

    Certain cards are tied to a specific civilization. Leader cards and capital cards, for instance, are civilization-specific. Other cards may be civilization-specific.

    The player must have civilization-specific cards belonging to exactly one civilization, as well as civilization-generic cards. At the least, the player must have a capital, which is civilization-specific.
